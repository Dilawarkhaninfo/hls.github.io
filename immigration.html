Immigration

Personal Matters

Our experienced immigration specialists provide support to private individuals.

Our expertise includes advising on:
  • applications for British citizenship
  • indefinite leave to remain applications in the UK (also known as settlement or permanent residence)
  • UK ancestry visas
  • spouse of a British national applications
  • the Investor category

We offer a full end-to-end visa application service which involves assessing your eligibility for the visa, advising on and reviewing the required documents, drafting any supporting letters and assisting with administrative tasks such as booking the biometric appointment.

Business / work Immigration matters

Sponsor licence applications

We can help you apply for a sponsor licence, which you need to hire foreign nationals. We offer a full service or a document review service. We can also do a pre-licence audit to make sure you understand the compliance requirements.

Audits

We offer a fixed-price audit to make sure you are complying with your sponsor duties. This includes reviewing your right to work checks to prevent illegal working and your HR systems to make sure you have the correct documents and processes for all employees. This is especially helpful if the Home Office notifies you (or shows up unannounced, which they are allowed to do) that they are coming to your business to do an official audit.

Training for ongoing compliance issues

We can provide training on a range of compliance issues, including completing right to work checks, navigating the Sponsor Management System, and advising on the documents that need to be kept on file for each employee. If you have any specific training requests, we can accommodate them and provide a customized session for you.

Policies, procedures, and HR processes

There are certain policies and procedures that employers need to follow when hiring foreign nationals. These include right to work check policies and monitoring visa expiry dates. We can review your current policies and advise on their effectiveness, or we can work with you to create new documentation.

Sponsored work visas

We can provide a full service to help you with the visa application process for any new hires, whether they are inside or outside the UK, and current employees who are extending their visas. We offer an end-to-end service and can liaise directly with your employees to ensure a smooth process. Alternatively, we can offer an ad hoc review of key documents before you submit your visa application.

Being a sponsor is a big responsibility. We can provide training and advice on the compliance requirements to help you maintain your A-rating licence.

Start-up visas

If you want to start a business in the UK for the first time, you may be eligible for a start-up visa. To be eligible, you need to have an innovative, viable, and scalable business idea that is supported by an endorsing body approved by the Home Office.

We can help you connect with an endorsing body and discuss your eligibility. Once you have been endorsed, we can help you with the visa application process. This includes reviewing and advising on your documents.

The start-up visa is not a route to settlement, but you may be able to switch to the innovator visa after 2 years.
